Moonlight Resort, two-star hotel in lakeside Pokhara, is a compelling theater to view fabulous vistas of Phewa Lake and Fishtail mountain, overlooking the Phewa Lake with the Annapurna range at the back drop. Moonlight Resort offers clean and elegant accommodation, coupled with the genuine friendliness of family run hotel. We have air conditioning and hot water system.


Situated between street 5th and 6th in the lakeside. This hotel is one of the most reasonable hotel in lakeside with a great staff and good breakfast. The manager and the staffs are very friendly and always ready to hear out suggestions. When I visited on the 1st week of March they were renovating the hotel and now it looks much better and homely. They never hesitated to help me out and called for the cab when i had to go out. And even booked my bungee, paragliding and zip lining. Loved the hotel and would definitely recommend it to my friends.
